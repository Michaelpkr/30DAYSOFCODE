Day 1

Today is day one of a thirty day exercise. I'll be setting a two-hour timer, and then coding what I can within that window. The reason why I want to subject myself to this cruel and unusual punishment is simply to get the practice of creation, and publishing live code. With today being day one, my plan is to work on creating a container that scales on both mobile and desktop, with potentially a navigation bar.

At this point, I don't even know where it's going to navigate to, so I guess we're on this adventure together. Buckle up.

Looks like these notes are going to be a stream of conciousness, and what I've decided that the navigation is going to give you, the wonderful reader, a time-lapse look at this thirty day journey. I've created a page for every day. Click through to see this project's progress.
